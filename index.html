About Us

Poor Helping Welfare Society is a nonprofit organization dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged individuals and communities. The NGO focuses on social welfare initiatives including education, healthcare, and poverty alleviation across various regions of India. It aims to empower marginalized groups through sustainable programs and community-driven development.

Our Mission

To uplift poor and marginalized communities by providing access to essential resources and opportunities.

Our Vision

To build a society where every individual can live with dignity, access quality education, and enjoy basic healthcare and livelihood opportunities.

Why Did Piyush Singh Thakur Start Poor Helping Welfare Society NGO?

Piyush Singh Thakur started Poor Helping Welfare Society NGO with a simple but powerful vision: to support poor, needy, and underprivileged people who struggle to meet their basic needs.

After witnessing the challenges faced by economically weaker families, including hunger, lack of education, inadequate healthcare, and limited opportunities, he realized that many people need timely support and guidance. This inspired him to establish the NGO and create a platform where individuals, volunteers, and donors could come together to serve humanity.

The organization works to provide food, essential supplies, educational support, social welfare programs, and community assistance to those in need. Its mission is to promote compassion, dignity, and equal opportunities for every person, regardless of their background.

Our Programs & Projects

Poor Helping Welfare Society NGO is actively engaged in a wide range of social welfare, community development, and humanitarian activities across India.

Education Support Programs

Arranging free education for poor and needy children, providing essential school kits, study materials, and mentoring support.

Poor & Needy Assistance

Planning free food arrangements so that the poor do not go hungry, providing ration kits, and delivering community aid.

Health Awareness & Medical Camps

Organizing free health camps to provide direct health benefits, free medical treatment, check-ups, and medicines to poor people and senior citizens.

Women Empowerment Initiatives

Providing vocational skill training, supporting independent livelihoods, and helping in the marriages of poor girls from underprivileged families.

Environmental Conservation

Making people aware about planting trees to keep the environment healthy, organizing mass plantation drives, and working on local ecological preservation.

Blood & Organ Donation Drives

Organizing blood donation camps and running continuous propagation and awareness creation programs for lifesaving organ donation.

Our Core Community Mission & Ideals

National Unity & Integrity

Actively working towards maintaining and promoting national unity and integrity across all sections of society.

Discourses of Great Men

Making proper arrangements for propagating, spreading, and keeping alive the principles and teachings of great visionaries.

Emergency & Accident Relief

Providing assistance, rescue support, and immediate care for individuals in emergency situations like accident victims.

Animal Welfare & Protection

Extending active support, healthcare, medical treatment, and shelter arrangements for cows and other animals.

Social Justice & Truth

Firmly standing on the side of justice to oppose the wrong and strongly support the truth.

Community Development Projects

Running collaborative programs targeted at structural improvement and development in localized regions.
